Mr, Leonhart

Leonhart (レオンハート Reonhāto?)[4] is an Eldian who lived in the Liberio Internment Zone and is the adoptive father of Annie Leonhart. He had trained his daughter to be a Warrior candidate which later led to her selection as inheritor of the Female Titan's power.
