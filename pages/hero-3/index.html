Chainsaw Man
Denji

Denji is the titular protagonist of the Chanisaw Man manga and anime series. He is the main protagonist of the Public Safety Saga and the deuteragonist of the Academy Saga. As a young boy, he inherits his father's debts from the Yakuza. After meeting Pochita, he becomes the Devil Hunter for the Yakuza in an attempt to clear his debt.

He is then revived and can become the Chainsaw Man by pulling a cord on his chest. After meeting Makima, he became a Public Safety Devil Hunter as a member of Tokyo Special Division 4. Following Makima's death by his own hands, he resigned from his position as Devil Hunter but continues to fight as Chainsaw Man under the public eye, all the while being enrolled in Fourth East High School as a student. He is also the caretaker and adoptive brother of Makima's reincarnation, Nayuta.

Makima

Makima is a Devil resembling a human woman in her twenities of average build, and marginally above average height. She has long light red/pale auburn hair, ormally kept in a loose braid with bangs reaching just past her eyebrows and two longer side bangs that frame her face. Her eyes are yellow with multiple red rings within them, making them the only visible sign of her inhuman nature.

Makima is the main antagonist of the Public Safety Saga. She was the Chief Cabinet Secretary's Personal Devil Hunter who took Denji in as her human pet. She is later revealed to be the Control Devil who embodies the fear of control at the time. Following her death, she was reincarted as Nayuta.
