DEMON SLAYER

Kamado Tanjiro

Kamado Tanjiro is the main protagonist of Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba. He is a Demon Slayer Corps member, who joined to find a remedy to turn his sister, Nezuko Kamado, back into a human and to hunt down and kill demons, and later swore to defeat Muzan Kibutsuji, the King of Demons.
